Dr. Eric Vero is a SSHRC Doctoral Fellow studying disability and fandom through a digital lens. He publishes non-fiction about the Mad and queer experience under the name E. A. Vero. His dissertation, (<a href=''>https://uwspace.uwaterloo.ca/items/20200081-69ba-4a2e-9e3b-78ae0e988880/</a>), told the story of how disabled fans fight ableism through sharing and shaping stories.
